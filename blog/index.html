First Speech LLM with unifed continuous tokenizer for both understanding and generation: Ming-UniAudio is an end-to-end unified speech language model with a single LLM backbone for both understanding and generation tasks, enhanced with a Diffusion Head to ensure high-fidelity speech synthesis.
