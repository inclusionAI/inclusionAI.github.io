Ling
The Foundation Language Model
Ling  is a MoE LLM model provided and open-sourced by InclusionAI. We introduce two models of different sizes, Ling-lite and Ling-plus. Ling-lite has 16.8 billion parameters, of which 2.75 billion are activation parameters; while Ling-plus has 290 billion parameters, of which 28.8 billion are activation parameters. Both models show impressive performance compared to existing models in the industry
Reasoning Derivation of the Foundation Model
Ring
Ring  is an inference MoE LLM derived from Ling, provided and open sourced by InclusionAI. We use a joint training process combining knowledge distillation and reinforcement learning to achieve comparable performance to the state-of-the-art (SOTA) baseline small inference model on the challenge with only one-third of the parameters activated
Multi-Modal Understanding and Generation
Ming
Ming-Omni  is a unified multimodal model that can process images, text, audio, and video. More importantly, Ming-lite-omni goes beyond traditional multimodal models and supports audio and image generation. Ming-lite-omni uses a dedicated encoder to extract tokens from different modalities, which are then processed by Ling
